FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our speech therapy services

We work with clients of all ages, from young children to adults. Our therapy approaches are tailored to each age group's specific needs and goals. Our therapists have specialized training in pediatric, adolescent, and adult speech therapy techniques.

The duration of therapy varies depending on individual needs and goals. We'll provide a detailed treatment plan after the initial assessment. Some clients may benefit from short-term therapy over a few months, while others with complex needs might require longer-term support.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us to verify your coverage and discuss payment options. Our administrative team is happy to help you understand your benefits and what may be covered under your specific plan.

The initial assessment typically takes 60-90 minutes. We'll discuss your concerns, medical history, and goals, followed by standardized assessments and informal observations. After the assessment, we'll develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.

Session frequency depends on your needs and goals. Most clients start with weekly sessions of 30-60 minutes. As progress is made, we may adjust the frequency. We work with your schedule to find convenient times for consistent therapy.
